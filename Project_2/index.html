My personal interests

Growing up my parents noticed my emerging talent in drawing and painting. They enrolled me in an art school and later I attended the National University of Art in Bucharest, Romania. I later transferred to Brigham Young University and completed a BS in Illustration. During my time at BYU I have taken an HTML class DigHT 250: Web Publishing and found that I really enjoyed coding too.

I appreciate a wide variety of art and was fortunate enough to see great works of art in museums across the United States. One of my favorites is Irises by Vincent van Gogh at the Getty Museum in Los Angeles, California. Art history is one of my favorite subjects because it blends history, culture, architecture, and music.

However, art did not provide financial stability, and I transitioned into IT. I hope to grow into an SDET role and beyond. This is why I decided to return to school and build strong technical skills at UVU.
